TECHCORP INC. — Q4 2025 QUARTERLY FINANCIAL REPORT

Executive Summary

TechCorp Inc. delivered strong financial results in Q4 2025, with revenue reaching $4.2M, representing a 23% increase year-over-year. This growth was driven by our expanding AI product suite and successful international expansion into Latin American markets.

Financial Highlights

Revenue: $4.2M (Q4 2025) vs $3.4M (Q4 2024) — 23% YoY growth
Operating Margin: 18% (up from 13% in Q4 2024)
Net Income: $756K (up 45% YoY)
Cash Position: $8.1M (healthy runway of 18+ months)
R&D Investment: $1.2M (29% of revenue)

Customer Metrics

Total Active Customers: 2,340 (up from 2,000 in Q3)
Customer Satisfaction Score: 4.7/5.0 (all-time high)
Net Promoter Score: 72 (industry average: 45)
Customer Retention Rate: 94%
Average Revenue Per Customer: $1,795/year

International Expansion

Brazil operations launched in September 2025 — contributing $320K in Q4
Colombia pilot program started November 2025 — $184K in initial revenue
Combined LATAM revenue: 12% of total company revenue
Target: 25% of revenue from international markets by Q4 2026

Key Risks and Challenges

1. Market Competition: Three new competitors entered our segment in Q4, including one backed by $50M in Series B funding
2. Currency Risk: BRL and COP volatility affecting LATAM margins (hedging strategy under review)
3. Talent Acquisition: Engineering hiring taking 45 days average (target: 30 days)
4. Infrastructure Costs: Cloud computing costs grew 31% vs 23% revenue growth

Strategic Priorities for Q1 2026

1. Launch AI Analytics Dashboard (beta) — target: 500 beta users
2. Expand sales team by 8 headcount (4 LATAM, 4 US)
3. Implement currency hedging for BRL/COP exposure
4. Achieve SOC 2 Type II certification
