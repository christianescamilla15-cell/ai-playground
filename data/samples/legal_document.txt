MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Agreement Number: MSA-2026-0042

BETWEEN:
Provider Corp ("Provider"), a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 100 Innovation Drive, San Francisco, CA 94105
AND:
Client LLC ("Client"), a Texas limited liability company with principal offices at 200 Commerce Street, Austin, TX 78701

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Provider shall deliver cloud computing, data analytics, and AI-powered business intelligence services ("Services") as described in each Statement of Work ("SOW") executed under this Agreement. Initial SOW value: $240,000 annually.

2. TERM AND RENEWAL

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term of 24 months. The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides 90 days written notice of non-renewal.

3. FEES AND PAYMENT

3.1 Annual License Fee: $240,000 (payable quarterly at $60,000)
3.2 Implementation Fee: $45,000 (one-time)
3.3 Support Fee: $36,000 annually (15% of license fee)
3.4 Payment Terms: Net 30 days from invoice date
3.5 Late Payment Penalty: 1.5% per month on outstanding balances

4. LIABILITY

4.1 The Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed $500,000.
4.2 Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.
4.3 The limitations in this section shall not apply to breaches of confidentiality or intellectual property infringement.

5. TERMINATION

5.1 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice for convenience.
5.2 Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach that remains uncured for 15 business days after written notice.
5.3 Upon termination, Provider shall return all Client data within 30 days.
5.4 Early termination fee: 50% of remaining contract value.

6. INDEMNIFICATION

6.1 Client shall indemnify and hold harmless Provider against any third-party claims arising from Client's use of the Services.
6.2 Provider shall indemnify Client against claims of intellectual property infringement related to the Services.

7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1 All proprietary information, trade secrets, and business data shared under this Agreement shall remain confidential.
7.2 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for a period of 5 years.
7.3 Exceptions: publicly available information, independently developed information, or information received from third parties without restriction.

8. NON-COMPETE

8.1 During the term and for 12 months following termination, Client agrees not to develop or market a competing product using Provider's proprietary methodologies.

9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

9.1 All disputes shall first be submitted to mediation.
9.2 If mediation fails within 30 days, disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration under AAA Commercial Rules.
9.3 Arbitration shall take place in San Francisco, California.
9.4 The prevailing party shall be entitled to recover reasonable attorneys' fees.

10. FORCE MAJEURE

Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters, pandemics, government actions, or infrastructure failures.

11. DATA PROTECTION

11.1 Provider shall comply with applicable data protection laws including CCPA and GDPR.
11.2 Provider shall maintain SOC 2 Type II certification throughout the term.
11.3 Data breach notification: within 72 hours of discovery.
